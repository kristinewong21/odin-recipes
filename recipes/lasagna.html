Famous Lasagna

Ingredients:

  • Tomato sauce
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozzerella Cheese
  • Egg

Steps:

  1. Boil noodles for 8 minutes
  2. Stack noodles, sauce, ricotta and egg mixture, and then cheese
  3. Bake for twenty minutes at 350 and allow to cool for 10 minutes before serving
  4. Enjoy!
